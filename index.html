iFlooder

v2.0.0 | iOS 14.0+

A powerful message flooding tweak for iMessage & SMS with full control over your spam.

Features

  • Fixed message spam
  • Word-by-word spam
  • Counting mode (1, 2, 3...)
  • Random emoji mode (25 emojis)
  • Adjustable speed (0.05s to 2s)
  • Message limit (auto-stop)
  • Live counter in navbar
Add to Sileo

Compatible with all arm64 devices on iOS 14.0 - 15.x
